WhatCal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

WhatCal ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered nutrition assistant that analyzes food photos to provide calorie estimates via WhatsApp. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Food Photos: Images of meals you send for calorie analysis
  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp phone number for communication
  • User Descriptions: Text descriptions you provide to improve analysis accuracy
  • Contact Information: Your name as provided in WhatsApp profile

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Frequency and timing of your interactions with WhatCal
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP address, and browser type
  • Analysis Results: Calorie estimates, nutritional information, and AI-generated summaries

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: Analyze your food photos using AI to provide calorie estimates
  • Communication: Send analysis results and reminders via WhatsApp
  • Service Improvement: Enhance AI accuracy and develop new features
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. AI Processing and Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our service:

  • OpenAI: Processes your food photos using GPT-4 Vision API for calorie analysis
  • WhatsApp Business API: Facilitates communication through Meta's messaging platform
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Stores your data securely in the cloud

These services have their own privacy policies and data processing agreements with us.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Location: Your data is stored on secure AWS servers
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your data
  • Retention Period: Data is automatically deleted after 30 days unless you request earlier deletion

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in providing our service
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to sharing

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 General Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
